School Teacher & Timetable Management System
Overview

This project is a centralized school management system designed to handle teacher data, student allocation, timetables, substitutions, and daily attendance in an organized and time synchronized manner.
It ensures smooth academic operations from Nursery to Class 12 by automating scheduling, substitution handling, and absence management.

The system reduces manual work, avoids timetable clashes, and provides real time visibility of teacher availability.

Key Objectives

Maintain complete records of teachers and students

Ensure proper synchronization of time, classes, and schedules

Automate substitution allocation during teacher absence

Provide transparency in teacher availability and free periods

Support advance planning and emergency handling
Core Features
Teacher Management

Stores details of all teachers including regular, extra, and substitute teachers

Supports addition and removal of teachers (new joinees and resignations)

Academic units are divided as:

Nursery to Class 5 as one unit

Class 6 to Class 12 as another unit

Tracks subjects, assigned classes, and weekly workload

Student and Class Management

Maintains class wise student records

Assigns teachers to respective classes

Ensures every class has a complete weekly timetable

Timetable Management

Generates weekly timetable for every class

Generates weekly timetable for every teacher

Prevents scheduling conflicts

Synchronizes teachers, rooms, and time periods

Maintains details of available classrooms

Attendance and Presence Tracking

Daily updates of teacher presence and absence

Real time status of teachers currently present

Displays list of teachers free at the current time

Shows free periods for each teacher

Absence and Leave Portal

Teachers can declare planned absence at least one week in advance

Reason for absence must be provided

Emergency absence supported with letter submission through the portal

Absence information automatically triggers substitution planning

Substitution Management

Automatically allots substitute teachers

Uses rotation logic to ensure fair substitution distribution

Utilizes extra teachers and teachers with free periods

Maintains balance between primary and secondary academic units

Ensures no class is left unattended

Real Time Availability Dashboard

Displays teachers free at the current time

Shows free periods clearly

Helps administration quickly assign substitutions

Displays available classrooms

Workflow Summary

Admin sets up teachers, classes, rooms, and subjects

Weekly timetables are generated for teachers and classes

Teachers submit planned absences in advance

Emergency absences require letter upload

System automatically assigns substitutes

Daily presence and absence data is updated

Dashboard shows real time teacher and room availability
